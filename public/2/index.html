How It Works

Our Platform

When a user launches the app for the first time, the user is prompted to sign up.

The app uses the GPS on the phone to detect the location. After which, there’s a display of several categories where the user can choose from. The user makes a choice, proceeds to checkout and places the order. A call is then placed to app user to confirm the order.

Restaurants

Restaurant receives a confirmation and starts preparation of the order prompted to sign up.

There is an app designed for the restaurants that shows the order of the customer from our platform with an identification code. The restaurant receives a confirmation and starts preparation of the order

Biker

The bikers have an app designed for them as well. Here the location of the app user is displayed.

The biker’s app also carries the identification code of the order. The restaurant matches the identification number of the order and hands over the food to the biker for delivery. The biker uses the maps on the app or pre-existing road network knowledge to navigate the customer’s location and delivers
